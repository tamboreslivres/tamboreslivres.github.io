STEWARDS NEEDED FOR CARNIVAL!

Volunteers needed to steward with Tambores Livres at Notting Hill Carnival on Monday 26.08.19. Be part of carnival for free!

Stewards hold a rope around the parade group, protecting our perimeter, alongside 2 dedicated NHC official stewards and police liaison officer. A plain green tee-shirt provided.

Tambores Livres, “Free Drums”, is a cultural collaboration bringing together Afro-Brazilian musical and cultural groups to parade together at Notting Hill Carnival 2019. In a Carnival first, 7 groups from the UK and Brazil are performing as one, to celebrate unity and raise awareness for democracy, human and environmental rights, justice and equality in Brazil.
