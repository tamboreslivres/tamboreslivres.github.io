Hi and thanks for joining the project! Here is the programme of events for carnival weekend.
Saturday 24th August 11am-4pm Final Dress Rehearsal and main T-shirt Collection
Sunday 25th August 1-4pm Tambore Livres Bands Tribo and Baque de Axe parading and rehearsing
Monday 26th August 8/9am-5pm Notting Hill Carnival Main Parade

Location

Maxilla Social Club, 2A, Maxilla Walk, London W10 6NQ. Nearest tube: Latimer Road

General information

You are welcome to come along and get involved with the activities on the Saturday, to help the final preparations and get your all-important T-shirt! There will also be a final combined band rehearsal to watch and that should get you in the mood for the main parade Monday! T-shirts can also be picked up Sunday at Maxilla and Monday (8am).

On the Monday, the meeting time is earlier – 9:00am, if you have your t-shirt, or 8:00am if you don’t. Roads will be closed around the carnival area and tubes may be busy.

Clothes

keep an eye on the weather. Ideally white trousers and white trainers but anything else is ok. We want the T-shirt on top so it can be worn over a warm layer or two if it is looking cold , or a waterproof if wet. Please add carnival make-up and hair wear if you want! Small waist bags are ok to wear but no rucksacks please as they obscures the logo on the t-shirts. Belongings/valuables can be left in a locked van if required.

Water & Food

There will be water to take from stewards on the parade route but bring a bottle if you want and if it looks like hot weather.

We don’t supply any food but there will be opportunities around the parade for a food stop and plenty of nearby carnival food stalls.

Safety

There will be a number of Tambores Livres stewards around the parade group - protecting our perimeter and helping with water - plus two dedicated NHC official stewards and Police liaison officers. We have a first aider in case of any minor injuries or issues. As with any big public event, please be aware that there will be large numbers of people around, especially as we finish the parade so just be aware of things like phones and wallets etc and keep an eye on the crowds as you pass through back to the Maxilla club.

Have a great Carnival and let’s create a big noise for our heroes and the TL cause. Brazil we are watching you!
